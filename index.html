Snacksman

He just may be the hungriest man on earth..

Coming to theaters in June 2016, a young boy who grew up with a pension for crunchy things both savory and sweet grows into a man and continues to snack his heart out like a small animal.

In the year that he turns 23 years and his appetite becomes even more insatiable, Snacksman begins to realize that his roommates hate him because he eats all the snacks. Will Snacksman be able to curb his appetite and re-earn their affection or will his addiction to Snacking be the demise of his social life?

A Universal Pictures production starring David Hasselhoff as...SNACKSMAN.

Even this man's appetite would be no match for Snacksman..
